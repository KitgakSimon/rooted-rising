About Us

What we do?

The Rooted Rising Initiative seeks to address the environmental and socio-economic challenges faced in the world. The initiative aims to build a just, inclusive, and sustainable society where everyone can thrive and achieve self-actualization.


Our mission is to ignite awareness, change narratives, educate, and inspire action among all stakeholders through various means, such as podcasts, multimedia stories, and community engagement, we aim to address the pressing issues affecting the world by promoting environmental conservation, socio-economic development, and community resilience to achieve a just and sustainable society where everyone can thrive.
