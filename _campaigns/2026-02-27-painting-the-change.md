Rooted Rising Initiative is proud to announce the live painting sessions of “Painting the Chage”, a powerful visual art experience in collaboration with the University of Port Harcourt Art Museum and The Young Environmentalists Network (TYEN) 

Painting the Change is an interrogation of environmental destruction, women body autonomy, the politics of extractivism, and the hope of impacted communities.

This session will take place at the University of Port Harcourt Art Museum, Abuja campus, Choba, Rivers State, Nigeria. 

Opening: March 6 | 10:00 am
